How to Make Onion Burger:

Material / Ingredients : 1,5 lb ground beef
1 tea spoon salt
0,25 tea spoon pepper
2 tea spoon mustard
6 split buns
1,5 onion, chopped
4 tea spoon butter
2 tea spoon horseradish

Steps :
First, prepare all the material you need Second, mix and blend ground beef, salt and pepper. After that, shape it into 12 small and thin patties. Then, brown chopped onion lightly in butter and add horseradish, mustard and salt. Next, spread onion mixture over 6 patties, top each with a patty and press together. After that, place all patties on grill and brown each side for approximately 8 to 10 minutes. At last, serve it on buns.
